# Security policy

See [docs/security.md](docs/security.md) for the full threat model.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email `security@goderash.dev`. Include:

- A clear description of the issue
- Affected components and versions
- Steps to reproduce (ideally a minimal PoC)
- Your contact info for follow-up

We respond within 2 business days.

## Responsible disclosure

Please do not open public issues for security-sensitive findings. We will
coordinate a disclosure window with you once the fix is ready.
